Pumpkin Soup

Pumpkin soup is a creamy, It combines the natural sweetness of pumpkin with savory aromatics and spices, creating a harmonious balance of flavors

Preparation time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 55 minutess
  • Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 medium sugar pumpkin
  • 2 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves minced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream (or coconut milk for a vegan option)
  • 1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg (optional, for warmth)
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional, for a subtle kick)

Instructions

  1. Prep the Pumpkin (Skip if Using Canned)
    • Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Cut the pumpkin in half, scoop out seeds, and place cut-side down on a baking sheet.
    • Roast for 35–40 minutes until tender. Let cool, then scoop out flesh (≈2 cups)..
