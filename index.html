Pumpkin Soup

Pumpkin soup is a creamy, It combines the natural sweetness of pumpkin with savory aromatics and spices, creating a harmonious balance of flavors

Preparation time:

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 55 minutess
  • Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium sugar pumpkin
  • 2 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves minced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream (or coconut milk for a vegan option)
  • 1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg (optional, for warmth)
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional, for a subtle kick)

Instructions:

  1. Prep the Pumpkin (Skip if Using Canned)
    • Peel the pumpkin, remove seeds and stringy bits, and cut into 1-inch cubes.
  2. Sauté Aromatics
    • Heat olive oil/butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add diced onion and sauté for 3–4 minutes until translucent. Stir in minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Cook the Pumpkin:
    • Add pumpkin cubes to the pot. Pour in stock, ensuring the pumpkin is mostly submerged. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for 20–25 minutes until pumpkin is fork-tender.
  4. Blend the Soup:
    • Let the soup cool slightly. Use an immersion blender to purée until smooth (or blend in batches in a regular blender). Caution: Allow steam to escape if using a blender to avoid splatters.
  5. Season and Finish:
    • Return the soup to the pot (if using a blender). Stir in cream/coconut milk (if using). Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Simmer for 2–3 more minutes. Add water or stock if too thick.
  6. Serve:
    • Ladle into bowls. Top with roasted seeds, herbs, or a swirl of cream. Serve with crusty bread.
